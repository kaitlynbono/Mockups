How it works

  1. Upload any image — the tool reads its exact pixel dimensions.
  2. Photopea opens in a new tab sized to match your image.
  3. Your image is placed as the Base Image (bottom layer).
  4. A Title text layer (Playfair Display, 72 pt, centred) is added on top.
  5. An Editable Elements layer group is created for your additions.
  6. The project is saved as a PSD and exported to your Downloads folder.
